About the Fashion Contest

Welcome to the Myntra Fashion Contest, a premier platform for upcoming fashion designers to showcase their creativity and talent! Our contest is designed to provide a stage for emerging designers to shine and gain recognition in the fashion industry.

We believe in nurturing new talent and offering exciting opportunities for growth. By participating in our contest, you stand a chance to win amazing prizes, gain exposure, and even land job opportunities with top fashion brands.

How to Participate

  1. Submit Your Designs: Create and submit your original fashion designs through our online submission portal.
  2. Showcase Your Talent: Your designs will be showcased to our customers who will vote for your designs within a week timespan.
  3. Win Prizes: The top designers will receive fantastic prizes, including cash awards, job opportunities, and more.

Prizes and Opportunities

Winners will receive:

  • Prizes: Attractive monetary awards for the top three winners.
  • Job Opportunities: Potential job offers from leading fashion brands.
  • User-Interaction: One-on-one sessions with customers to guide them and design dresses for them according to their demands.
  • Exposure: Your designs will be featured on our platform and promoted through our social media channels.
